# Tiger Credit Card - Knowledge Base

Grounding facts for the voice agent. The agent may ONLY state facts from this document or from injected system variables.

## Product Details

- **Joining fee:** Rs 499 one-time, lifetime free after
- **Cashback rates:**
  - 10% on top shopping brands (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra)
  - 5% on travel platforms (MakeMyTrip, Yatra)
  - 1% on everything else, including UPI payments
- **Cashback currency:** Jewels (5 Jewels = Rs 1), redeemable as statement credit
- **Welcome rewards:** Rs 500 cashback + 1-year Amazon Prime worth Rs 1,499 (credited on activation)

## KYC Process

- **eKYC:** Available anytime, takes approximately 2 minutes, done on phone via deep link
- **VKYC (Video KYC):** Available 9 AM to 9 PM IST, takes 2-3 minutes, customer shows ID on camera
- **Card activation:** One-tap in app, instant virtual card, physical card dispatched in 5-7 days

## Compliance Boundaries

The agent must NEVER:
- Read out full PAN, Aadhaar, or card numbers
- Promise credit limit increases not backed by system data
- Pressure the customer after they decline
- Make calls outside 9 AM to 9 PM IST
- Proceed without consent verification
